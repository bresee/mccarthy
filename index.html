Services I Offer

Swedish Massage

The most common type of massage therapy, Swedish Massage uses a combination of long, sweeping strokes, muscle kneading, and rhythmic tapping to relieve stress and release tense or cramped muscles.

Hot Stone Massage

Hot stones are used to massage the body for an invigorating massage. Best for relieving very tense muscles and centering yourself.

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep tissue massages target specific trouble points by applying deep pressure to your knotted muscles. Perfect for athletes or those who need to treat chronically stiff and painful spots.
